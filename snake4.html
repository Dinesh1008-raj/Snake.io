HOW TO PLAY

  • Guide the snake using arrow keys or on-screen controls
  • Eat food (golden circles) to grow longer and earn points
  • Each food gives you 10 points
  • Every 5 foods increase your speed level
  • Avoid hitting the walls or the snake's body
  • Longer snakes move faster - be careful!
  • Press SPACE or the PAUSE button to pause/resume
  • Press START to begin or RESTART after game over
